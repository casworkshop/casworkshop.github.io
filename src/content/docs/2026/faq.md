---
title: Frequently Asked Questions
---

Q1. Is work on sentiment/valence welcome or does the submission have to include affect/emotions?  
A: We welcome work on all aspects of emotions and affect, including sentiment, polarity, and valence.

Q2: Does the submission have to include ML/AI/LLMs?  
A: No, this is not a requirement. we welcome all kinds of scholarly work on studying affect and emotions through data.

Q3: Can the submission include an Appendix in the main pdf or should it be a separate document?  
A: Appendices can be included in the main pdf (after references).
